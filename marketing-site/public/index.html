Why Tamma?

The Problem

Development teams spend 40-60% of their time on repetitive, low-creativity toil—writing boilerplate tests, fixing linting errors, addressing review comments, and managing CI/CD coordination. Existing tools provide basic automation without end-to-end orchestration, are tightly coupled to specific platforms (GitHub-only solutions), or lack the transparency teams need for production use. Business stakeholders fear autonomous systems making breaking changes without control, while open-source maintainers struggle with review burden that doesn't scale.

The Solution

Tamma is a hybrid autonomous development platform that orchestrates complete development workflows across 8+ AI providers and 7+ Git platforms through intelligent abstraction layers. It provides complete transparency through CQRS event sourcing, enabling time-travel debugging and audit compliance. The 14-step autonomous loop completes 70%+ of development issues without human escalation while maintaining strategic checkpoints at business decisions, breaking changes, and deployments. Smart quality gates prevent issues rather than just detecting them, with mandatory escalation after 3 retry attempts ensuring the system never gets stuck.

Unique Value

Tamma's MVP goal is self-maintenance—the platform must be robust enough to maintain its own codebase autonomously, demonstrating production-readiness for mission-critical software. This dogfooding approach validates that Tamma can handle complex, real-world codebases safely. Combined with multi-provider flexibility (no vendor lock-in), complete audit trails for compliance, context-aware modes (Dev Mode for speed vs Business Mode for control), and prevention-first quality gates, Tamma enables 3x productivity gains by reducing repetitive toil from 40-60% to under 20% of development time.
