Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tamma ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website tamma.dev (the "Site").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you voluntarily provide it to us:

  • Email Address: When you sign up for launch notifications through our email signup form

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We use privacy-respecting analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics) that may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Referring website addresses
  • Geographic location (country/region level only)

Note: Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies or track individual users. All data is aggregated and anonymized.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Launch Notifications: To send you updates about Tamma's Alpha launch and product announcements
  • Site Improvement: To understand how visitors use our site and improve the user experience
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

Your email address is stored securely in Cloudflare KV storage with the following protections:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Access controls limiting who can view subscriber data
  • Regular security audits

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Cloudflare for hosting and storage services
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your email address from our mailing list
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from our emails at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@tamma.dev.

7. Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies for tracking purposes. Cloudflare Web Analytics is a privacy-first analytics solution that does not use cookies or collect personally identifiable information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. Cloudflare operates a global network of data centers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@tamma.dev
  • GitHub: github.com/meywd/tamma
